# Roadmap

## Status

This roadmap covers currently approved baseline milestones only. It does not promise delivery dates.

## Current Milestones

- Open-source foundation and legal separation
- Monorepo and development environment
- Core data and authentication foundations
- Ingestion, review, and retrieval workflows
- Orchestration, citation, and user experience
- Operations, evaluation, and release readiness
