# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, review comments, chat rooms, and any project-hosted events or channels.

## Expectations

Members of the community should:

- Be respectful and precise in technical discussion.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or humiliating language.
- Protect privacy and confidentiality when handling user reports, source materials, and review artifacts.
- Escalate security, legal, and religious-content concerns through the documented private workflows.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, threats, or repeated intimidation.
- Doxxing, disclosure of private conversations, or publishing sensitive evidence without permission.
- Deliberate misinformation, credential sharing, or distribution of restricted datasets.
- Sabotaging review, moderation, or safety workflows.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers through the private contact path listed in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) when the issue overlaps with security or privacy.
For general conduct concerns, use the maintainer contact path described in [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md).

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, pause review privileges, or restrict participation when behavior threatens the safety, privacy, or integrity of the project.
